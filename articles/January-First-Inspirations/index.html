Musing over - Daily Inspiration from the Monk who sold his Ferrari

Today’s inspiration is about Impact and Legacy; and about Personal Greatness. January 1 deals with disconnect current civilizations are facing among it’s members.

Here are my musings over this topic. These are original, so handle with care and respect.

Cry and World Laughs, Die and World Cries.

You might have born crying, try to laugh when dying.

World might have rejoiced when you were born; but make sure it cries when you’re dying.

Take time to know your neighbors.

Neil Armstrong might be the first man on the Moon (and most famous man on Earth), but most important people in your life live near you.

In modern society, our neighbors hold keys to our problems; unknowingly.

I blame online social networks for the disconnect we have among fellow human beings today. How can anything virtual provide you with benefit of real touch, warmth and laughter of people around you?

Skype, GMail, Instagram, FaceBook and similar more are keeping us from becoming social beings that we can be.

Our future generations will look back at us & wonder that why we chose to become machines?
