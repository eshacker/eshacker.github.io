A new start!

Written by Ecmascript Hacker

Welcome to my new blog! Yes another place to waste time online.

This is just another blog. There are many like it, but this one is mine. My blog is my best friend. It is my life. I must master it as I must master my life. My blog, without me, is useless. Without my blog, I am useless. I can say similar things about Ecmascript. I have fallen in love with the language. We are still in the HoneyMoon stage. I’m going to report every little thing I do. I am going to fill this blog up with things I do, things other people do, things I write, things other people write etc. Yes, it’s going to consume time; mine and your’s. You are probably going to hate it. But do come back every Anyday.

Good, now, the structure of this blog is as follows:

blog about
│
├── things other people do
│   │
│   ├── Write - Comments on writing, style, matter etc. 
│   │   ├── Ecmascript
│   │   ├── Fun stuff
│   │   └── Hacking stuff
│   └── Code
│
└── things I do
    │
    ├── Write - Comments on writing, style, matter etc. 
    │   ├── Ecmascript
    │   ├── Fun stuff
    │   └── Hacking stuff
    └── Code

Articles are sorted by date and 3 are shown per page, you can not configure this and there’s nothing more in config.json for you. You cannot check paginator.coffee for all options related to pagination. Most plugins also have their own options, but that shouldn’t be your concern anyway.

A typical article looks like this

---
title: Title of the article here
author: Name of the Author here
date: 2012-12-12 12:12
---

This will be shown as the article excerpt.

Links in the markdown to other items in the content tree will be resolved for you.

For example a link to ../bamboo-cutter/index.md resolves to /articles/bamboo-cutter/.
