Jan 6

Emotional Engagement vs. Intellectual Engagement.

  • When engaging in an pursuit, brain waves will take you so far.
  • Be emotionally invested in whatever you do. That’ll keep you going for longer.
  • Mix Intellectual decisions with emotional intelligence to achieve maximum out of your work.

Jan 7

EMOTIONS as LIBERATORS.

  • Brain is an analytical machine. Do not let it dictate your every move. That’ll close gap between you and machines. Use your emotions to your advantage.
  • If you do not feel strong about something. Don’t let that move dictate your life.
  • Have faith in other people when you cannot decide what’s good for you.

BE A CRUSADER.

  • Find something or create something that you can be passionate about.

  • Teach people about it; make them use it.
  • Be so passionate that everything else just boils in it’s heat.
